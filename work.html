Work

Below you can find a selection of some of my projects

  • Soviet Grass

    This band from Bruges needed a simple promotional website for their "Plastic Fingers" EP Release. The website design follows the EP design and has an online form linked to a CRM where people were able to order tickets for the release party. Another part of the project was to create print tickets with a tear-off strip.

  • dfind

    Dfind is a vintage and contemporary design gallery featuring furniture, lighting and interiour decorating items, located in the heart of Bruges.
    I worked out the website design with a strong focus on SEO and the whole website is updateable with an easy to use CMS.

  • Rob Michiels

    Rob offers a fine selection of rare ceramics from around the world. With several museums and important private collections as clients, it is a continuous journey across the world to find the rarest items of fire and earth. Rob gave me an empty canvas to design the website on, and everything is updateable with an easy to use CMS.

Older projects

Before going freelance full-time, I had the opportunity to work on interesting projects at my previous employer, Trinch Webstudio.

I had the opportunity to work for clients like Bosch, Filliers, Grinta, De Lijn, Latexco, Belgian Skyshops, Haco, Genico, Obumex, and many more...

I also was the lead developer for the Trinch CMS, which was completely written in-house for maximum control over tailoring and security needs. I also designed and coded the current Trinch.com website.
