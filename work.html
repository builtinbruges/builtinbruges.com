Work

Below you can find a selection of some of my projects

  • Soviet Grass

    This local band needed a simple promotional website for their "Plastic Fingers" EP Release. The website design follows the EP design and has an online form linked to a CRM where people could order tickets for the release party. Print tickets for the show also had to be made and also follow the EP design.

  • dfind

    Dfind is a vintage and contemporary design gallery featuring furniture, lighting and interiour decorating items, located in the heart of Bruges.
    I worked out the website design with a strong focus on SEO and the whole website is updateable with an easy to use CMS.

  • Rob Michiels

    Rob offers a fine selection of rare ceramics from around the world. With several museums and important private collections as clients, it is a continuous journey across the world to find the rarest items of fire and earth. Rob gave me a free canvas to design the website on, and everything is updateable with an easy to use CMS
